New Agricultural Policies for 2024 Fiscal Year Announced

John Doe
News 5 min read

The Ministry has officially unveiled a comprehensive package of subsidies aimed at promoting sustainable farming practices across the northern and eastern regions.

In a press conference held this morning, the Minister outlined the key pillars of the new initiative, which includes financial grants for organic fertilizers, state-sponsored training programs for water conservation, and tax incentives for small-scale farmers adopting solar irrigation systems.

"This is not just about increasing yield," the Minister stated. "It is about ensuring the longevity of our soil and the prosperity of our farming communities for generations to come."

Key Highlights

  • 30% subsidy on organic compost.
  • Zero-interest loans for solar pump installation.
  • Regional workshops starting November 1st.
